Privacy Policy — VideoSnap Hub (速去印)

Effective Date: 2025-11-10 · Company: Nicevdo

Overview

VideoSnap Hub (速去印) is developed by Nicevdo. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Information We Collect

  • Device Information: model, OS version, language, and screen resolution.
  • Identifiers: Advertising ID for ads and analytics.
  • Usage Data: app events, crash logs (via Firebase Crashlytics).
  • Network Data: IP address and network status for performance optimization.
  • User-Provided Data: optional data such as email or feedback.

Permissions (Android)

PermissionPurpose
INTERNETTo download videos and display ads.
ACCESS_NETWORK_STATECheck network connection before download or ad requests.
READ/WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGESave downloaded files (Android ≤ 12).
READ_MEDIA_VIDEO / READ_MEDIA_IMAGESAccess video files for saving or sharing (Android 13+).
POST_NOTIFICATIONSNotify users about download progress.
FOREGROUND_SERVICEKeep downloads running even when minimized.

Advertising & Analytics

  • Google AdMob — Provides in-app ads.
  • Firebase Crashlytics — Collects crash data for app stability.
  • OneSignal — (Optional) For push notifications.

Children’s Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

US State Privacy Rights

We comply with CCPA, CPRA, CDPA, CPA, CTDPA, and other major state privacy laws. You may request access, deletion, correction, or opt-out by emailing addregis@gmail.com.

Data Retention & Security

We keep crash and analytics data for up to 12 months. We use encryption and limited access to protect your data.
